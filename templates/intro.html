The Bank of America Chicago Marathon is the pinnacle of achievement for elite athletes and everyday runners alike. Runners from all 50 states and more than 100 countries will set out to accomplish a personal dream by reaching the finish line in Grant Park. This prestigious race is limited to 45,000 runners and registration is driven by a general lottery system. However, this year, the Bank of America Chicago Marathon has reserved 35 guaranteed spots for Asha for Education's Team Asha runners. You can secure your spot in this prestigious race and become a Team Asha runner by filling up this registration form.

Run for Education

All Team Asha runners participating in the Bank of America Chicago Marathon will be committed to raise at least $1000. These funds help support education initiatives for underprivileged children in India. To date, over 99% of our runners have successfully met their fundraising target and have even exceeded it in some cases.
