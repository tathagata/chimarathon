Train. Run. Educate.

The Bank of America Chicago Marathon is the pinnacle of achievement for elite athletes and everyday runners alike. Runners from all 50 states and more than 100 countries will set out to accomplish a personal dream by reaching the finish line in Grant Park. This prestigious race is limited to 45,000 runners and registration is driven by a general lottery system. However, this year, the Bank of America Chicago Marathon has reserved 35 guaranteed spots for Asha for Education's Team Asha runners. You can secure your spot in this prestigious race and become a Team Asha runner by filling up this registration form.

Run for Education

All Team Asha runners participating in the Bank of America Chicago Marathon will be committed to raise at least $1000. These funds help support education initiatives for underprivileged children in India. To date, over 99% of our runners have successfully met their fundraising target and have even exceeded it in some cases. Suggested fundraising target for students is $800. The target can be further lowered by getting your friends to run for Asha!

Why run for Asha?

Asha for Education's mission is to catalyze a socio-economic change in India by educating underprivileged children. In the last 24 years, Asha for Education has helped shape more than 50,000 lives through 400+ educational projects across India. By running the marathon for Asha you are helping us take that message to the world and your efforts will directly help to kindle curiosity and bravery in the minds of a number of young Indians.

How does your fundraising help?

A sum of 300 USD will help one child for a year to do the following things:

  • Buy books, pencils, crayons, educational toys
  • Buy school uniform
  • Buy food supplies for the child in residential centers
  • Enable the child attend personality development workshops
  • Enable the child to participate in educational field-trips for hands on learning

How can Asha help you?

Asha is a world wide community of motivated individuals who are dedicated to helping others. We have a number of veteran runners, who have ran in previous marathons for Asha since 2011. We will help you by
  • Info sessions throughout the training period (nutrition, hydration, equipment, training, etc.)
  • Mentors to guide through the training and fundraising process
  • Happy hours and team get-together with fellow runners and Asha members
  • Team Asha running t-shirt to wear during the race
  • Pre-race dinner party
